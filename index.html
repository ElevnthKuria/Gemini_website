About Us

Gemini Trading Agencies Ltd specializes in providing comprehensive logistics solutions, including clearing and forwarding, import and export services, and warehousing. Our mission is to ensure seamless and efficient goods movement across borders, tailored to support both businesses and individuals.
Since our establishment in 2016 , we have successfully handled close to 5,000 units , building a strong reputation for reliability, efficiency, and customer satisfaction in Kenya, Rwanda, Congo and Sudan. At Gemini Trading Agencies Ltd, we take pride in our team of 65+ skilled drivers and a dedicated group of motivated professionals who work tirelessly to ensure seamless and high-quality services. Our commitment to precision, timely deliveries, and cost-effective solutions has made us a trusted partner for businesses across East Africa.
